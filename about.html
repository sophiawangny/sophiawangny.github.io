Hello World! I am Sophia, a UX Designer and Researcher from Queens, NY. I am a third-year computer science and art student at Brandeis University. I want to build products that understand people, and use creativity and problem-solving skills to fuel my passion for assisting others.

scroll for more







When I was little, my biggest hobbies were to sit by the window, doodle all day, and google all the questions I had about the world. As answers magically appeared before me with a press of the "enter" key, my constant curiosity sparked my love for technology and art.

Fast forward to today- and I am still in love with those same things. My goal is to build products that understand people. I want to be intentional about the things I put out in the world. In the big picture, I hope to dedicate the work I do towards assisting others and creating an impact. Many problems can be solved through technology, but it's about how we go about solving them, and how much empathy and purpose we put into providing those solutions. Generating experiences that are mindful and can be enjoyed by everyone is something that is important to me.

I am a passionate listener, a creative technologist, a thoughtful creator, and a curious storyteller.
I am currently seeking internships in 2022! I hope we could work together soon.
