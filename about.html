Hello World! I'm Sophia. I currently study computer science and art at Brandeis University. I am a proud New Yorker, born and raised in Queens, NY. I use my creativity and problem-solving skills to fuel my passion for assisting and impacting others.

When I was little, my biggest hobbies were to sit by the window, doodle all day, and Google random questions I had, watching as the answers magically appeared before me. Growing up during a time of rapid technological change, curiosity of my surroundings fueled my love for technology and art.

Today, I am still in love with those same things. Though I am older now, I do not have a set plan for my life, and I do not intend on having one. What I do know about myself is enough, and it's this- I want to pour myself into doing work that goes towards assisting others and creating an impact. I want to provide solutions to problems that can be solved through technology, having it be mindful and inclusive, generating experiences that can be enjoyed by all. I am a strong advocate for the growing tech field to create with the intentions of providing accessible and usable products.

I am a passionate listener, a creative technologist, a smart designer, and a curious creator.
