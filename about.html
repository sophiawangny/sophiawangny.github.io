Hello World! I'm Sophia. I currently study computer science and art at Brandeis University. I am a proud New Yorker from Queens, NY. I use my creativity and problem-solving skills to fuel my passion for assisting others.

When I was little, my biggest hobbies were to sit by the window, doodle all day, and google all the questions I had about the world. As answers magically appeared before me with a press of the "enter" key, my constant curiosity sparked my love for technology and art.

Fast forward to today- and I am still in love with those same things. Though I am older now, I still do not have a set plan for my life, and I do not intend on having one. What I know about myself is enough, and it's this- I want to pour myself into doing work that goes towards assisting others and creating an impact. I want to provide solutions to problems that can be solved through technology, having products be inclusive, generating experiences that can be enjoyed by everyone. I am a strong advocate for the growing tech field to create with the intentions of making products mindful and accessible.

I am a passionate listener, a creative technologist, a smart designer, and a curious creator.
I am currently seeking internships in 2021! I hope we could work together soon.
