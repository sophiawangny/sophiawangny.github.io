Hello World! I'm Sophia. I currently study computer science and art at Brandeis University. I am a proud New Yorker, born and raised in Queens, NY. I strive to use my passions for creativity and problem-solving to mold unique and accessible experiences for everyone.

Ever since I was little, I loved to stay in with my crayons and just doodle all day. At the same time, I was growing up during a time of rapid technological change, where curiosity fueled my love for technology.

I want to be a creator in the vast field of technology - but not one who will generate products that can work only for certain populations when they are meant for everyone. I want to create products in tech to be mindful and inclusive, ones that generate experiences that can be enjoyed by everyone. I hope to be an advocate for products that are accessible and usable for all.

I am a passionate listener, a creative technologist, a smart designer, and a curious creator.

I am Sophia Wang.
