What:

As an executive board member of Brandeis University's computer science club, BITMAP, I helped organize Brandeis' first ever online hackathon. Along with researching resources to create an inclusive online event, I was in charge of Codestellation's logo, sponsorship package, and designing + developing the hackathon website. Check it out here.





The Codestellation 2020 Sponsorship Packet was sent out and pitched to potential sponsors. We were able to be sponsored by Wolfram, the Brandeis Maker Lab, Hiatt Career Center, etc.
