Overview

Scattering Resources, Inc. (SR) is a non-profit organization that draws attention to the needs of individuals in poverty in Haiti and in the U.S. A significant portion of SR’s efforts are focused on disadvantaged individuals in Haiti who have limited access to education, training and health care, and who are living, in some cases, in dangerous environments due to wars, natural disasters and unsanitary conditions.

This redesign is currently in progress, but here is what we have done so far :-)

Problem

Whether it is donating, volunteering, or accessing
resources, the Scattering Resources site is not being
engaged with as much as the non-profit's other
forms of outreach into the community is. The other
forms include social media, emails and calling, which is inefficient and difficult to organize at times

Approach

  • Interviews with resource recievers and volunteers
  • Soft Audit
  • SiteMap
  • Cognitive Walkthroughs
  • Bi-weekly client meetings
  • Design Reviews

    • Goals

  • Promote engagement with site
  • Have quick and easy sign ups/registration
  • Provide easy-to-read resources
  • Have clear, understandable, and rich visual text content and structure

    • Process

    Research -> Ideate -> Design -> Test -> Iterate


    Research

    Interviews with SR volunteers and clients

  • Signing up for resources like workshops and webinars is not clear
  • Information on resources is difficult to read
  • Not much informational advice-oriented content in resources
  • Sign ups for volunteering is confusing
  • Simple and brief information is often preferred and desired on front
  • Donate option is hidden in site

    • Soft Audit

  • A lot of empty space on sides not used, with small percent of content
  • No integrated donation page on the website
  • Site settings in other languages should be offered (for Haitian users: french, Haitian Creole)
  • Previous events held is not displayed
  • Social media buttons are small
  • "Our Work" pagename doesn't describe page content --> switch to "Resources" or "Our Services"
  • "Our Work" and Donation Button disappears when resizing tab’s width, creating invisibility
