K H A L I D   R E B R A N D

Graphic Designer

What:

   Creative Project

When:

   Feb-May 2021

Tools:

   Adobe Creative Suite
In the Spring semester of 2021, I cross-enrolled in Boston University's Design&New Media Class, MET CM 744. Tasked to create rebranding for the musical artist of our choice, I took on 23 year-old musician, Khalid, who became popular at the young age of 17, writing about life as a teen in Al Paso, Texas. Using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, I created a unique, fresh, and meaningful rebranding for Khalid.

Target Audience

  • American teenagers, ~17-22 year old

    • Demographic Lifestyle

  • The everyday teen with everyday problems with love, family, and finding purpose in life
  • They have free time to hang out, think about life, commute to school, etc.

    • Music Genres

  • R & B
  • Pop
  • Rock
  • Hip Hop
  • Soul

    • Color Palette

    After examining Khalid's music, past albums, style, and audience, it was quickly noticed that Khalid's brand includes lots of blue and orangish-yellow.
  • Khalid's identity is represented in the colors -- His past albums seem to mostly take place in settings of of blue skies and orange deserts, which is the setting of El Paso, where he resonates with the most.
  • Khalid has a mixture of slow and upbeat pieces
  • The blue is cool and relaxing, but can also represent the slow and sad songs he writes/sings
  • The orange represents the more upbeat, fun, catchy songs he has

    Closing Summary

    Overall, the goal of this rebranding was to add uniqueness in Khalid's brand, and most importantly, resonate with Khalid listeners to feel nostalgia, relaxation, and refresh. Khalid's music is something that people can relax and bop to for hours, and having a brand experience that fit the nostalgic music experience that he creates is crucial in making the artist as a whole stand out and be recognized clearly.
